About Us


We are a small company with a passion for video games and electronic entertainment systems. This includes mobile gaming to PC gaming and console gaming whether its Playstaton or Xbox.
We pride ouselves in delivering top quality and honest reviews of games so you know what to buy and what to avoid. It doesnt matter what level you play at, wether you are a video gaming enthusiast, a new comer to gaming, a casual gamer or a professional player in Esports we are certain you will enjoy our reviews and help you find the next best game to add to your library.

We try our best to be as open minded with our reviews as possible and try to find the best qualities as well as the worst qualities in a game.
We try not to show favouritism but a few of our favourite game developers are Bethesda Sotfworks, Rockstar and Paradox.

A Little Piece of History

Our passion started in the early 2000's when a group of friends started writing reviews for each other about their favourite games at the time. Our reviews soon became popular in school and we started writing reveiws for the school paper to the whole school and we was able to reach out to more and more people.
Eventually we left school but our fun didn't end there, we soon founded our own webiste in 2009 and our reviews grew on a larger scale. Our fame grew and suddenly game publishers and developers started contacting us to review their game, small companies at first but it didn't take long the the main stream developers to take notice.
In 2011 our big moment arrived as the game developer giant Bethesda Softworks contacted us and requested we review their newest title "The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim". We was shocked by this and jumped at the opportunity. Ever since then we have been reviewing AAA games with the approval of the big gaming developers and our clientbase and our business grow.
